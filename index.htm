Our Story

Rooted in Passion,
Refined by Time

Bella Vista was born from a simple belief: that great food is the language of love. Founded in 2010 by Chef Marco Rossi, our kitchen has always been a place where classical technique meets seasonal imagination.

Every dish is built from relationships — with local farmers, artisan producers, and the guests who return season after season to see what we've created for them.

The Bella Vista Difference

An Experience Like No Other

Three pillars guide everything we do — from kitchen to table.

Master Chefs

Our culinary team trained in the world's finest kitchens — from Paris to Tokyo. Every plate is an expression of decades of mastery and relentless curiosity.

Fresh Ingredients

We source daily from local farms, sustainable fisheries, and artisan producers. If it isn't seasonal and impeccably fresh, it doesn't reach your table.

Cosy Atmosphere

Warm candlelight, hand-crafted furnishings, and curated acoustics create an ambiance that feels intimate for two and celebratory for twenty.
